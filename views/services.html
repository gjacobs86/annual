Services Offered

The Open Architecture Collaborative, as a global network, has over 15 years of volunteer management, creative placemaking coordination, and community engagement experience. We’re developing our programs with our partners and aim to build community capacity and serve as an intermediary between professional practitioners and systemically marginalized communities. Community members, firm principals, and individual volunteers we look forward to hearing from you!

Architecture or Design Firm wanting to build community design capacity? (Professional Leadership Development)

Many design firms are beginning to see the benefit of Pro-bono services, either on their own or by participating in the 1+ program through Public Architecture. However, we have found that community practice requires a different skill set than traditional design education offers. We are working with firms in the San Francisco Bay Area to teach emerging designers the necessary skills and knowledge for community focused practice.

Reach out to discuss trainings

Community Development Corporation or community association looking to Launch Engagement or Creative Placemaking Programming?

The OAC can help you run community driven design/build projects and creative placemaking programs to engage your neighbors and further invest in their environments. We can support your community group or nonprofit with a specific project while building capacity for this type of civic engagement along the way.

The OAC with currently partnered with the East Bay Asian Local Development Corporation (EBALDC) to support their community engagement and placemaking efforts. The OAC, as a community design consultant, is producing a 4 month community driven design/build program while simultaneously training 7 architects and designers in the skills and knowledge of community engaged design. The result will be six small scale projects, ranging from murals to a pop-up library, and more active and supported neighbors of the North West Oakland area around San Pablo Ave.

Email us to chat about engagement
